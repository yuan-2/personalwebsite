Project Description

This was my first ever web design project. We were tasked to come up with a website as a solution to an issue in the world today.

As university students who are living comfortably, we realised our privileges - having access to information whenever we need it e.g. through the internet, with the help of seniors, etc.

Thus, we decided to come up with this mentor-mentee matching programme where current university students can sign up as a mentor to a less-privileged student who is considering to attend the same university.

Project Description

For this project, we were tasked to design a solution to a real life problem currently. We decided to propose a fun solution to the stricter travelling rules due to COVID-19. HelloWorld is a game that allows user to travel around the world at the comfort of their homes.

Every month, a virtual treasure (cash prize) will be placed in a certain country. Every day, a player will receive a hint to the treasure's location. They will have 3 chances each day to guess the location, and will also be able to explore the country virtually with Google Street Maps.
